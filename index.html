Alessandra G. Strickland started her ballet studies at the State School of Dance Maria Olenewa in Rio de Janeiro. After winning awards in many International Ballet Competitions such as CBDD, Dance Festival of Joinville, Trujillo, Dance Seminar of Brasilia, she won a scholarship at the age of 13 to continue her ballet education in Europe at the State Ballet School of Berlin- Germany. She then graduated from the Ballet School of the Vienna State Opera and was a trainee with the Ballet Company in Austria. Alessandra danced professionally in Ballet Companies in Europe, US and Brazil.

Alessandra is an American Ballet Theatre Certified Teacher, who has successfully completed the ABT Teacher Training Intensive in Primary through Level 5 of the ABT National Training Curriculum. She has many years of teaching experience in professionals and private Schools, in all levels and in coaching young dancers for ballet Competitions and Repertoire.

She is passionate about ballet and loves to share her knowledge with young dancers of all ages!

Mission

The Mission of AGS Ballet Aspen is to provide students with the highest standards of ballet training in a fun learning environment, nurturing confidence and discipline. I wish that the Fine Art of Classical Dance inspires all my students throughout their lives as it inspired me!

Classes

Primary Level- Class for young dancers- ages 4 to 6. Students need to be ready for a class experience without a parent in the classroom. Class will meet once a week for 45 minutes. In this level students will be introduced to a traditional ballet class structure, emphasizing movement fundamentals and principles, such as correct posture and musicality. Class cost $ 70 for a 4 week session or $120 for a 7 week session.

Level 1- Class for young dancers- ages 6 to 8, with previous ballet experience. At this level students will be introduced to the ballet technique, to ballet positions of feet and arms, body directions, continuing to apply movement fundamentals and principles. Class length of 1 hour, meeting once a week. Class cost of $120 for a 7 week session of classes.

Policy: there will be no refunds for missed classes. If you would like to schedule a makeup class for a missed class, please contact Ms. Alessandra to schedule depending on availability at agsballetaspen@gmail.com.
